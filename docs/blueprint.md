# **App Name**: paBondi

## Core Features:

- Featured Stores: Display a curated/random selection of active stores with name, logo, and brief description on the homepage.
- Featured Products: Showcase featured products with name, price, and image on the homepage.
- Store Listing: Enable users to browse all available stores with filtering and search options.
- Store Details: Display detailed information for a single store, including products offered.
- Product Listing: Allow customers to browse all products, with filtering and search.
- Product Details: Present detailed product information, including images and store association.
- Shopping Cart: Implement a client-side shopping cart to add and manage products, persisting state.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A muted deep sapphire blue (#2B3990) for interactive elements, chosen for its sophisticated and trustworthy feel.
- Background color: An inviting off-white (#F4F4F8), derived from the primary hue with low saturation, to provide a clean backdrop.
- Accent color: A desaturated blue (#4A6990) to highlight secondary information with elegance and restraint.
- Modern sans-serif fonts for headings and body text.
- Clean lines and generous whitespace to ensure a clutter-free interface and enhance the user experience across devices.
- Use minimal and consistent icons throughout the app.
- Incorporate subtle, tasteful animations to enhance user interaction and provide feedback, without being distracting.
